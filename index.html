About Me

My name is Chris Perez, and I am a web developer and content marketing specialist living in Seattle. I value simple design, intuitive UI, engaging UX, and content worth sharing.

I jumped into the world of software development because I love languages. I studied formal linguistics at The University of Arizona and spent 5 years as a copywriter and editor for a marketing firm. I also love to solve problems by building things--from tables to sound systems. As a developer, I get to build new products and solve interesting problems, all while learning new languages and nit-picking syntax.

Need a product built or a problem solved?

I'm available for freelance, contract, and consulting.

If you are trying to get a project off the ground, looking to take your website to the next level, or hoping to reach more of the right kind of customers, I can help! Send me a message to tell me about your needs, or just to share a clever annecdote.
